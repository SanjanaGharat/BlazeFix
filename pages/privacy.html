Privacy Policy

Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect data within the Forest Fire Prediction System. By using our services, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Updates to Our Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our technology, legal requirements, or operational needs. Users will be notified of any significant changes.

Information Collection

We collect various types of data, including:

  • Environmental data from meteorological agencies and satellites.
  • Sensor-based data from IoT devices deployed in forest areas.
  • Historical fire data and weather patterns.
  • User interactions with our system.
  • Location-based information to enhance fire predictions.

Use and Sharing of Information

The collected data is primarily used to:

  • Improve wildfire prediction accuracy.
  • Generate early warnings to mitigate risks.
  • Share insights with governmental and environmental agencies.
  • Conduct research and enhance our machine learning models.

We do not sell personal data and ensure that shared data is anonymized whenever possible.

Analytics

We use analytics tools to monitor system performance, analyze trends, and improve prediction models. These tools help refine fire risk assessments and optimize alerts.

Data Sharing

Data may be shared with:

  • Government agencies responsible for disaster management.
  • Environmental organizations for wildfire research.
  • Meteorological institutions to improve forecasting accuracy.

All shared data is either anonymized or provided in aggregate form to protect user privacy.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to support predictive modeling and improve system effectiveness. Users can request data deletion in accordance with our policies.

Security

We employ robust encryption methods and security protocols to safeguard data from unauthorized access, cyber threats, and breaches.

Your Choices

Users have control over data sharing preferences within our platform. Non-essential data collection can be opted out of at any time.

Third-Party Sites

Our system may integrate with third-party platforms for additional data sources. We are not responsible for their privacy policies and encourage users to review them separately.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Continued use of the system after updates constitutes agreement to the revised terms.

Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at blazefix4@gmail.com.

Effective Date

This policy is effective as of the publication date and will remain in effect until further notice.
